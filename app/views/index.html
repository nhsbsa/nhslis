  • Users want clearer info around what part of their rent they need to tell us about, if they get help towards it.
  • They need clearer feedback that the application has been submitted and what the next steps are.
  • Users who are low on the digital inclusion scale could use this, but require the correct language to understand it.
  • Known issues:
  • No error messages in the prototype.
  • No feedback when sections are done.
  • Uses dummy data on summary screens.
  • Users don't understand benefits and use different names for them.
  • We need to understand how often people pay for things like rent and be more explicit about monthly, four weekly vs calendar month.
