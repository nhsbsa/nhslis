What we found:

  • Users we tested with (low digital skills) found the sign in confusing. They seemed to be more inclined to use a memorable word.
  • They were overwhelmed by the sign in pages, there is too much being asked for at once.
  • The page that tells users what they will need to apply is not direct enough. One of our users was confused by the title Low Income Scheme, 'I'm not on the low income scheme' - she thought it was a type of benefit. Many of the users don't know what the low income scheme is.
  • Several users tried to select the whole row on the LIS home screen.
