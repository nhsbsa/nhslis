What we've changed:

  • Refined the entitlement pages.
  • Improved what you will need to apply page.
  • Improved save and continue / memorable word / application number pages.
  • Improved contact preference questions.
  • Refined the journey for application number & memorable word.
  • Added journey for users to get a copy by email or in the post.
  • Shows a list of the people you have told us about.

What we found:

  • Some users didn't realise didn't they needed to go to their entitlement here.
  • Users were able to get through the service.
  • Users with ISA's would like to see the term used so they know where to enter the information.
  • Users are confused about benefits, pension credit can be a stumbling point.
