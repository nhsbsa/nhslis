Low Income Scheme

To apply we need to ask you about the following:

  • income you receive, including work, pensions or benefits
  • income your partner receives
  • mortgages, rent or your council tax you pay
  • property, land, bank or savings accounts you have
  • other people who live in your household

You should have this information ready before continuing.
