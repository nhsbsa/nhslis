Apply for help with health costs

The low income scheme

You could get help towards:

  • NHS Prescriptions;
  • NHS Dental treatment;
  • sight tests, glasses and contact lenses;
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports;
  • travel to receive NHS treatment (when referred by a GP or hospital doctor, dentist or optician)

If you have a low amount of money to live on after your housing costs and you or your partner (or both) have less than:

  • £16,000 in savings, investments or properties (not counting the place where you live); or
  • £23,250 in savings, investments or properties if you permanently live in a care home

You can apply at any time. Don't wait until you need treatment.

You may already be entitled to help with health costs if you receive any of the following:

  • income support;
  • pension credit guarantee credit;
  • universal credit; or
  • you are named on, or entitled to, an NHS Tax Credit exemption certificate

What to do if one of the above applies to you

You can claim for:

  • yourself;
  • your partner; or
  • your children
