2. Help for people who are pregnant or have had a baby in the last twelve months

Maternity exemption Pregnant women and those who have had a baby in the last 12 months get free: NHS prescriptions - but only if you have a valid maternity exemption certificate NHS dental treatment if, when you are accepted for a course of treatment, you are pregnant or have had a baby in the previous 12 months.

people who are pregnant or have had a baby in the last twelve months

You may:

  • May get help towards the cost of repair or replacement of glasses or contact lenses
  • May get help with travel costs for NHS treatment.
  • NHS dental treatment for any course of treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports
