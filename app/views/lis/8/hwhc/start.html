1. Introduction to help with health costs

If you receive treatment in an NHS hospital this is usually free but there are a number of things which there may be charges for.

You may be able to get help with:-

  • prescription charges
  • NHS dental charges, including check-ups
  • sight tests
  • the cost of glasses and contact lenses
  • travel costs to and from hospital for NHS treatment
  • travel costs if travelling abroad for treatment
  • wigs
  • fabric supports, for example support tights.

You will need to show evidence to prove you are entitled to help.
