Sight tests

Show this certificate to the person who tests your sight and ask if you are entitled to any help with the cost.

If you are, ask for a form to apply for this help. You then pay whichever is less:

  • the amount specified on your certificate, plus any difference between the NHS fee and the private sight test cost, or
  • the actual sight test cost.

    • The person who tests your sight can tell you how much the NHS sight test fee is. Eye examinations are free to all in Scotland
