Before you start

You're not eligible for help if you or your partner (or both) have more than:

  • £16,000 in savings, investments and properties

    (not including where you live)

    • or

  • £23,250 in savings, investments and properties

    (if you permanently live in a care home)

You're already entitled to help (and don't need to complete this application) if you recieve:

  • income support
  • pension credit guarantee credit
  • universal credit

    • or

  • if you are named on or entitled to an NHS Tax Credit Exemption certificate
