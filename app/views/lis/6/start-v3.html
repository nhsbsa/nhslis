Low Income Scheme

You may be entitled to help towards the cost of:

  • NHS Prescriptions
  • NHS Dental treatment
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports
  • sight tests, glasses and contact lenses
  • travel to receive NHS treatment

You're not eligible for help if you or your partner (or both) have more than:

  • £16,000 in savings, investments and properties

    (not including where you live)

    • or

  • £23,250 in savings, investments and properties

    (if you permanently live in a care home)

You can apply any time (not just when you need treatment) for:

  • yourself
  • your partner or children
